FAQs

Common questions before booking.

Is this only for women in tech or sales?

Not at all. While Cheryl has deep experience in those worlds, she works with ambitious women across many industries. What matters most is where you are — not what field you're in.

How long does a typical engagement last?

It depends on the work and the woman. Some clients start with a single Clarity Session and go from there. Ongoing coaching engagements typically run 3–6 months, with flexibility built in.

Do you work with clients outside the US?

Yes. All sessions are virtual and Cheryl works with clients internationally. Time zone logistics are always sorted in advance.

What if I'm not sure coaching is right for me?

That's exactly what the consultation is for. There's no commitment required, and Cheryl will be honest with you about whether this is the right kind of support for where you are.

How is this different from therapy?

Coaching is forward-focused and action-oriented. We work on where you're going and what's getting in the way — not on processing the past. Many clients work with both a therapist and a coach simultaneously.

What does it cost?

Pricing is discussed during the consultation and varies by engagement type. Cheryl's work is a premium investment — and it's designed to deliver a return that goes well beyond what you pay.
