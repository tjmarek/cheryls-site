My Story

I've lived the burnout.
Now I guide others through it.

I'm Cheryl Donahue — career sales executive, mentor, wellness advocate, and coach for women who are ready for their next chapter.

After 27 years of leadership, two company exits, a breast cancer diagnosis, and my own burnout and reinvention, I understand the cost of chasing success without a compass. I've rebuilt from the inside out — and I help other women do the same.

My approach blends executive strategy with real human warmth. No fluff, no generic playbooks — just clarity, honesty, and a "we'll figure this out together" mindset.
